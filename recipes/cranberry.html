Cranberry Chutney

A sweet chutney, perfect for Thanksgiving and Christmas. It's delicious on meats.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh cranberries
  • 1 cup chopped apples
  • 1 orange peel, chopped
  • 2 cups white sugar

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, mix the cranberries, apples, orange peel, sugar and water.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Boil 5 minutes, or until the berries are soft
